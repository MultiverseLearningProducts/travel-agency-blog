5 things to do in Kyiv

  1. Explore the historic charm of Kyiv's Old Town: Take a stroll through the cobblestone streets, admire the stunning architecture of St. Sophia's Cathedral and St. Michael's Golden-Domed Monastery, and immerse yourself in the rich history of this ancient city.
  2. Take in panoramic views from the Kyiv Pechersk Lavra: Visit this UNESCO World Heritage site, also known as the Kyiv Cave Monastery, and marvel at the beautiful golden domes and intricate frescoes. Climb the bell tower for breathtaking views of the city skyline.
  3. Discover the vibrant food scene: Indulge in traditional Ukrainian cuisine by sampling delicious dishes like borsch (beetroot soup), varenyky (dumplings), and holubtsi (stuffed cabbage rolls). Visit local markets to taste fresh produce, homemade sweets, and try the famous Kyiv cake.
  4. Wander along Andriyivsky Descent: Known as the "Montmartre of Kyiv," this historic street is lined with art galleries, craft shops, and quaint cafes. Admire local artwork, pick up unique souvenirs, and soak in the artistic atmosphere as you stroll through this bohemian neighborhood.
  5. Experience the nightlife on Khreschatyk Street: Explore Kyiv's main boulevard, filled with bustling cafes, lively bars, and trendy nightclubs. Join the locals as they unwind, socialize, and dance the night away, making unforgettable memories in the vibrant heart of the city.
